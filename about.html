    Kommunity (Midnight Touge in 2016-2019) is a discord server made in 24th November of 2016 by the idea of AnNoobTuner, MUTUN90 & N0RTHERNR0GUE in Drift Rouge Track. Kommunity used to be a public discord server meant for car enthusiasts to hang out in and have fun in but ever since 2019 everything has changed. Midnight Touge is changed to Kommunity and the car community "Motorway" was born because of the movie Motorway. Now, Kommunity is a private hangout server and members inside continue to make memories together
